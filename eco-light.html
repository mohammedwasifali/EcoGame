How to Play

• The room devices turn ON when no body is in the room
• Use the left and right switches to turn them OFF
• Each time a lit device is turned OFF, +10 points are awarded.
• The timer runs for 60 seconds. Aim for as many OFF actions as possible!

Great job!

Every switch matters. Turning off unused lights and electronics saves energy, reduces emissions, and lowers bills. Small habits add up—make it a routine.
